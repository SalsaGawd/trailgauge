Welcome to TrailGauge
A trail tool for off-road drivers and hikers. Pitch and roll readings, weather, GPS, trip recording, and saved pins — all built for the dash or the trail.
🚙 🥾
Two Modes
Off-Road shows the inclinometer with tilt alarm and max-angle tracking.

Trail hides the gauge and surfaces a compass, weather, and elevation as the focus.

Switch anytime in Settings.
📍
Calibrate & Drop Pins
ZERO/CALIBRATE sets your phone's resting angle as the reference. Always recalibrate when you change the mount.

Tap the coordinates at any time to drop a pin or open in maps.
