Welcome to TrailGauge
A trail tool for off-road drivers and hikers. Pitch and roll readings, weather, GPS, trip recording, and saved pins — all built for the dash or the trail.
🎯
First: Calibrate
Mount your phone, then hold ZERO/CALIBRATE for 1.5 seconds to set your resting angle as the reference.

Re-calibrate any time you change how the phone is mounted.
📍 📸
Pins & Trips
Tap the 📍 coordinates chip to drop a pin (add a photo for a shareable image card).

Hit START TRIP to record distance, elevation gain, and your full route — view and re-share later from the 📋 trips list.
💡
More Hints Will Appear
Other features (angle pins, ambient alerts, weather panel) will explain themselves the first time you encounter them with small inline hints.

Replay any hints any time via Settings → Show Hints Again. Have fun out there.
Send Feedback
Found a bug, have an idea, or just want to say hi? Drop a note — it goes straight to the developer.
